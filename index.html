Welcome to African Fellowship Ministry

We extend a warm and loving welcome to all visitors! At African Fellowship Ministry, we believe in the power of community, worship, and spreading the love of Christ. Join us for our uplifting Sunday service led by our beloved Pastor, Pr. Kissu Kipanga.

Our Sunday service begins at 2:30 PM and concludes at 4:30 PM. It's a time of spiritual enrichment, fellowship, and celebration. We invite you to experience the joy of worship and the warmth of our church family.

As we gather in His name, let us remember the words from the New Testament:

"A new command I give you: Love one another. As I have loved you, so you must love one another." - John 13:34 (NIV)

May this message of love inspire and guide us in our journey of faith. We look forward to worshiping with you!
